# How to Use Our Tailored Telegram Bot to Automate Operations

Integrating and using our tailored Telegram bot can streamline your operations by automating tasks such as managing expenses and incomes. This guide will help you sign in, obtain a Telegram Token, and configure the bot for seamless integration with your account.

![A person using Telegram Bot on their mobile phone](/blog/telegram-bot-integration/telegram.png "Telegram Bot")

### Step 1: Sign In to Your Account

To begin using our tailored Telegram bot, [sign in](https://app.monfuse.com/sign-in) to your account on Monfuse.

![Sign in page interface of the Monfuse finance app](/app/sign-in.png "Sign In")

### Step 2: Obtain Your Telegram Token

After signing in, you'll need to obtain your Telegram Token. This token is necessary to connect the bot to your account. Navigate to the [Automations page](https://app.monfuse.com/automations) and copy your prepared Telegram Bot Token.

![Automation page interface of the Monfuse finance app](/app/automation.png "Automation")

### Step 3: Paste the Token into the Bot Chat

With your token in hand, the next step is to connect your account with the bot chat. To do this:

1. **Start**: Click the "Start" button on the bottom of [chat](https://t.me/CredivioBot),
2. **Register**: Paste and send your token to the bot

The bot will automatically verify your token assign and assign your account to it. From now on, you can use the bot to add operations!

### Using the Telegram Bot for Automation

Our Telegram bot is designed to help you manage your financial operations efficiently. Here's how you can use it to automate various tasks:

#### Log Incomes and Expenses

1. **Text Entries**: Simply type the income or expense details into the chat, and the bot will automatically log them. For example, you can enter: <div className="my-4"><Copyable content="Spent $50 on groceries" /></div> The bot's response should indicate that the expenses has been logged to your account.

2. **Categorize Expenses**: The bot categorizes expenses for better tracking and reporting.

---

3. **Scan Receipts**: Send a photo of a receipt, and the bot will automatically extract the expense details and log them.

---

4. **Audio processing**: Send audio message with details of your recent payment, and the bot will automatically save it on your account.

## Conclusion

Using our tailored Telegram bot to automate your financial operations can save you time and improve accuracy. By following the steps outlined in this guide, you can easily integrate and start using the bot to manage expenses, incomes, and generate reports.

For further assistance, visit our [sign-in](https://app.monfuse.com/sign-in) page, obtain your [Telegram Token](https://app.monfuse.com/automations), and interact with the bot via the [Bot chat](https://t.me/CredivioBot).
