# How to Manage Your <TextBeam>Recurring Payments</TextBeam> with Monfuse? Guide for users

Managing recurring payments is crucial for financial stability. Monfuse simplifies this with its [recurring payments](/recurring-payments) feature, allowing users to track, schedule, and manage consistent inflows and outflows like bills, rent, salaries, or subscriptions.

## Why Use Recurring Payments in Monfuse?

1. **Track Consistent Payments:** Never miss a payment with due dates and notifications.
2. **Visual Dashboard:** View upcoming and past payments at a glance.
3. **Automation:** Reduce manual tracking—recurring payments update themselves.
4. **Telegram Notifications:** Stay updated on recurring payments when connected with the Monfuse Telegram bot.

## Managing Recurring Payments

Learn how to schedule, modify and delete recurring payments in Monfuse.

### Add a Recurring Payment

1. Click _Add_ Button:
   - Located above the [Active Payments](#active-payments) section.
2. Fill in Payment Details:
   - **Title**: Name of the payment (e.g., "Apartment Rent").
   - **Amount** and **Currency**.
   - **Interval**: Daily, weekly or monthly.
3. Save Changes:
   - Your payment will appear in the [Active Payments](#active-payments) list and sync with the calendar.

### Edit a Payment

- Locate the payment in the [Active Payments](#active-payments) table.
- Click the options menu (three dots) and select **Edit**.
- Update details like amount, frequency, or end date.

### Delete a Recurring Payment

- From the same menu, select **Delete** to remove the payment.

## Understanding the Dashboard

The Monfuse recurring payments dashboard is designed to provide a clear, organized overview of your recurring transactions. Each section offers tailored features that simplify financial tracking, improve transparency, and allow you to take full control of your payments. Here's an in-depth look:

![Recurring payments interface from Monfuse app with active, past, upcoming records and calendar](/app/recurring-payments.png)

### Upcoming Section

The upcoming section highlights payments that are due soon, ensuring you’re always prepared.

- Countdown Timers: Each entry includes a live countdown timer (e.g., “4 days 13 hours 14 minutes” for "Rental Income"), making it easy to prioritize upcoming payments.
- Early Preparation: This section acts as a reminder, helping you stay on top of deadlines and avoid missed payments.

### Active Payments

This section lists all ongoing recurring payments, giving you a consolidated view of transactions that are currently in progress.

- Detailed Table View: Displays key information such as title, amount, currency, last payment date, and the next scheduled payment..
- Payment Management: As discussed in the [Managing Recurring Payments](#managing-recurring-payments) section, you can edit or delete payments directly from this view.

### Calendar

The calendar provides a complete timeline of your payment history and schedules, blending past and future data into one seamless interface.

- Holistic Payment View: Payments are color-coded for quick identification (e.g., green for successful, yellow for upcoming).
- Date Selection: Select specific dates to view all payments scheduled for that day, making it easy to plan and review your financial commitments.
- Payment Scheduling: You can use the calendar to schedule a new payment by selecting the desired date and adding payment details directly.

### Latest Payments

This section offers a snapshot of your recently processed payments.

- Payment History: Displays the most recent transactions, including essential details such as payment source, amount, and date.
- Quick Access: Ideal for reviewing past financial activity or reconciling payments that have already been made.

This dashboard brings everything together—upcoming tasks, active transactions, and historical data—empowering you to take full control of your recurring financial obligations. With features like sortable columns, countdowns, and calendar scheduling, Monfuse delivers a comprehensive and intuitive financial management experience.

## Notifications for Recurring Payments

Monfuse ensures you stay informed about your recurring transactions by focusing on critical updates. Notifications are sent exclusively for **processed payments**, confirming that a recurring transaction has been successfully completed and recorded in your account. This ensures you only receive relevant, actionable updates, minimizing unnecessary alerts.

### Seamless Integration with Telegram Bot

For added convenience, if your Monfuse account is linked to the **Telegram bot**, notifications for recurring payments are also delivered via Telegram. You'll receive a clear summary once the payment is successfully processed. This integration provides instant updates, keeping you informed on the go.
